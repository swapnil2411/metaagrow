BUSINESS OPERATIONS MADE EASY

Metaagrow is an Operations and Maintenance Software, that ensures a transparent and controlled environment, driven with processes and systems.

For IOS For Android

Work- Life Before Metaagrow

  • Multiple Dashboards to monitor all locations.
  • Long, unjustified downtime on Assets.
  • No system dependency, more on staff.
  • No real time reporting.
  • No tracking mechanism on Cost and Assets.
  • Long desk hours.

Work- Life With Metaagrow

  • One Dashboard, with filters to monitor all locations.
  • Visibility now on all downtime of Assets with an escalation matrix.
  • Completely system driven, streamlined processes.
  • Linked with Tableau BI, for better analytics and decision based on facts.
  • Stored in Cloud, all in real time – Even if you go offline
  • You can now track your Cost, Assets & AMC’s.
  • Balanced work-family life.

FAQ's

What is Metaagrow application ?

Metaagrow application is a SaaS based application, empowering the front end staff on their very own smartphones / tablets to complete their daily tasks through checklists, which converts these checklist into analytical reports, all on one dashboard.

How do I start using Metaagrow application ?

Register on the Metaagrow platform on the website by filling out the necessary information and your contact details, selecting a plan of your choosing - a Metaagrow team member will get in touch with you.

Is there a lock-in or minimum commitment period ?

The standard plan is for a one-year subscription.

We use a biometric device for attendance ? Can you integrate with that ?

Yes, we integrate with most biometric devices.

Will you help with implementation ?

Yes, our onboarding team will help you at every step of the way including integration. However, a cost of integration may arise.
