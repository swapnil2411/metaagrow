OUR STORY - Metaagrow

Metaagrow was conceptualised by a seasoned team of professionals from the bowling, arcade gaming and amusement industry.

As a company we focused and addressed all the pain points while installing or operating centres across the country, we understood these pain points and have now inducted a solution to each pain point we faced in Metaagrow, bringing about better ease of use.

Vision

To be the most trusted and user friendly application used globally.

Mission

To be recognized as the preferred technology partner that maximizes the business value of our customers.

Core Values

We focus on the User and everything else follows. We always have Fun. We can make Money without doing Evil. We Dream Big. We Build Relationships.
